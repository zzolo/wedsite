Accomodations and Attractions

Lodging

We are currently working on more details about lodging. Do let us know if you have any problems finding a place. Some options for for lodging:

  • The nearest town to the wedding is Milaca, MN. There are a few hotels in this area.
  • The Mille Lacs State Park is only about 20 minutes away and has camping options.
  • The Grand Casino Mille Lacs Hotel is fairly close and will likely have a shuttle service to the wedding.
  • There are a number of vacation rentals on Mille Lacs Lake. Note that these may fill up quickly and keep in mind that is a very large lake.
  • St. Cloud is about 45 minutes away from the wedding but has many hotel options.
