Accomodations and Attractions

Near the wedding (central Minnesota)

We have blocked out rooms in hotels, but these are filling up. Do let us know if you have any problems finding a place to stay. The following places are near where shuttles will run (see Transportation seciton below).

  • The Grand Casino Mille Lacs Hotel is fairly close and will likely have a shuttle service to the wedding. We have reserved a block of rooms here; it should be under our names or Judy Tompke.
  • The Village at Izatys has some nice rentals and others have already made reservations there.
  • There are a number of vacation rentals on Mille Lacs Lake. Note that these may fill up quickly and keep in mind that is a very large lake.
  • The Super 8 in Milaca. Other hotels in Milaca like, the Phoenix Hotel.
  • Rum River Barn and Vineyard offers a camping area on site that can be used Thursday and Friday night. Please let us know if you would like to stay here so we can tell the venue.

Some other accomodation options that may not be that accessible to our shuttles.

  • St. Cloud is about 45 minutes away from the wedding but has many hotel options.
  • The Explore Minnesota site has a number of listing. Here is a search of places to stay within 50 miles of the wedding venue.
  • The Mille Lacs State Park is only about 20 minutes away and has camping options.

And there are many attractions throughout greater Minnesota:

  • Boundary Waters
  • North Shore
  • Duluth, MN
  • Isle Royale
  • ... and much more

In the Twin Cities

We live in South Minneapolis. There are few hotels in the City of Minneapolis besides downtown which can be a bit expensive. The surrounding subrurbs have more hotels and cheaper prices. We are very close to Richfield, MN. Do let us know if you have any problems finding a place to stay.

  • There are multiple, relatively cheap hotels near the Mall of America or around Richfield, MN. It is not central to Minneapolis, but only 15 minutes from where we live.
  • There are a number of hotels in downtown Minneapolis but these can be expensive.

There are lots of things to do in the Twin Cities:

  • Walker Art Museum
  • Mill City Museum
  • Science Museum of Minnesota
  • See music at First Avenue
  • ... and much more

Transportation

On the day of the wedding

Our goal is to make sure everyone has a good time, while still being safe and making sure there is no drinking and driving. Please, please let us know if you intend to drink and don't have a ride; we will be happy to find you a ride.

The following are the location for shuttles; please see the schedule and map for times and locations respectively.

  • Mille Lacs Casino,
  • Izaty's resort (the south side of Mille Lacs Lake),
  • and the Super 8 in Milaca.

Getting to central Minnesota

Unfortunately there is not good public transport to get from the Twin Cities to central Minnesota (around the wedding site). We suggest you rent and share cars if possible. If you are unable to rent a car, just want to save money, or otherwise can't find transport, please let us know and we can work something out.
