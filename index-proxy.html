Near the wedding (central Minnesota)

We are currently working on more details about this. Do let us know if you have any problems finding a place to stay.

  • The Grand Casino Mille Lacs Hotel is fairly close and will likely have a shuttle service to the wedding. We have reserved a block of rooms here; it should be under our names or Judy Tompke.
  • The Village at Izatys has some nice rentals and others have already made reservations there.
  • The nearest town to the wedding is Milaca, MN. There are a few hotels in this area like the Phoenix Hotel and the Super 8.
  • St. Cloud is about 45 minutes away from the wedding but has many hotel options.
  • There are a number of vacation rentals on Mille Lacs Lake. Note that these may fill up quickly and keep in mind that is a very large lake.
  • The Explore Minnesota site has a number of listing. Here is a search of places to stay within 50 miles of the wedding venue.

In the Twin Cities

We live in South Minneapolis. There are few hotels in the City of Minneapolis besides downtown which can be a bit expensive. The surrounding subrurbs have more hotels and cheaper prices. We are very close to Richfield, MN. Do let us know if you have any problems finding a place to stay.

  • There are multiple, relatively cheap hotels near the Mall of America or around Richfield, MN. It is not central to Minneapolis, but only 15 minutes from where we live.
  • There are a number of hotels in downtown Minneapolis but these can be expensive.

There are lots of things to do in the Twin Cities:

  • Walker Art Museum
  • Mill City Museum
  • Science Museum of Minnesota
  • See music at First Avenue
